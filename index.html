About Me

Hello! I'm an AI Engineer focused on building robust and responsible Generative AI solutions for the enterprise. At Lloyds Banking Group, I contributed to the foundational GenAI infrastructure, including the a "LLM Switchboard" as a core platform with its unified API for standardised model interaction.

Beyond the core platform, a significant part of my work involved developing specialised tooling for AI assessment. I researched, designed and built an automated red-teaming pipeline specifically for scanning LLMs against critical risks like hallucination, misalignment, and bias. This pipeline is crucial for comparative model analysis and significantly reduced a manual evaluation process from 14 days to under 3 hours, enabling faster, data-driven decisions on model suitability.

Separately, to address application-level assessment, I developed 'Pegasus', a performance evaluation package (available on my GitHub) tailored for assessing systems like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) applications based on key metrics.

These tools, combined with the standardised platform, support the bank's goal of deploying effective and reliable AI use cases. My background combines hands-on technical expertise in Python, evaluation frameworks, and GCP services with an analytical foundation from my Accountancy degree and ongoing Data Science studies.
